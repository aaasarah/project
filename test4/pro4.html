The Audi A4 allroad quattro.

Generous ground clearance, an extroverted design and a high degree of functionality – the Audi A4 allroad quattro combines many qualities. Thanks to the quattro drive, fitted as standard, the allroad-specific standard chassis, the optional damping control system and powerful TDI and TFSI engines, the vehicle is also suitable for trips off-road.



You wouldn’t think that using the electronically operated rear tailgate and retractable luggage cover could be any easier. But with sensor control, we’ve taken convenience to another level. For fast, convenient loading and unloading simply move your foot beneath the rear bumper to open or close the rear tailgate, without having to search for the key.
